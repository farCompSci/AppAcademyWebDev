What are Headings

Headings are emphasized texts placed in the tage `h1` up to `h6`. The higher the number, the smaller the text and the less it should be emphasized.


What are paragraphs

Paragraphs are bodies of texts, placed in the `p` tag, as mentioned previously. It can be used to store larger bodies of text will less emphasis than headers.


Lists

A list is pretty explanatory. There are two types: unordered and ordered. Ordered lists use the tag `ol` and unordered lists use the tag `ul`.
